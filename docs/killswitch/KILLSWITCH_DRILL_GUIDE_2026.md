## 3. Standard Drill Flow (6 Steps)

1. **Scenario Release** — Tuzi or rotating chair releases simulated crisis scenario 48 hours before drill
2. **Emergency Session** — All members enter meeting within 30 minutes (async permitted)
3. **Proposal & Vote** — Determine which level to activate; conduct formal vote
4. **Technical Execution Simulation** — Node-02-G / Node-01 executes corresponding scripts (system not actually shut down; execution results recorded only)
5. **Hot Wash** — Immediate review: what went well? what got stuck? how long did decisions take?
6. **Action Items** — Generate concrete improvement items; record in Drill Report
