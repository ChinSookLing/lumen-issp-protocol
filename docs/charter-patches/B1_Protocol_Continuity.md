## §12.1 協議存續條款 | Protocol Continuity Clause

【來源】M92 Emergency + M93-B1（6/6 unanimous）
【狀態】🔒 已鎖定

### §12.1.1 存續原則 | Continuity Principle

> Lumen ISSP 的存續不依賴任何單一個人、組織、平台或 AI 模型。協議一旦發布，即為獨立存在的公共知識資產。
>
> _The continuity of Lumen ISSP shall not depend on any single individual, organization, platform, or AI model. Once published, the protocol exists as an independent public knowledge asset._

### §12.1.2 不可撤回 | Irrevocability

> 已發布的 Protocol 版本（tagged release）不可被任何個體或實體單方面撤回、刪除或使其不可用。
>
> _No published Protocol version (tagged release) may be unilaterally withdrawn, deleted, or rendered unavailable by any individual or entity._

### §12.1.3 存續條件 | Continuity Conditions

> 只要以下任一條件成立，協議即視為存續中（alive）：
>
> _The protocol is considered alive if ANY of the following holds:_
>
> 1. 至少一個節點仍在運行且通過 Conformance Test（At least one node is running and passes Conformance Test）
> 2. 至少一個 Council 成員仍可存取 repo 並有權限推送（At least one Council member can access and push to repo）
> 3. 公開的 tagged release 仍可被任何人下載並重建（Public tagged release remains downloadable and rebuildable by anyone）

### §12.1.4 創始人不可用 | Founder Unavailability

> 若創始人（Tuzi）連續 14 個日曆天無法聯繫（定義見 §12.2），Council 自動進入 Emergency Protocol（§12.2）。創始人不可用不構成協議終止的充分條件。
>
> _If the Founder (Tuzi) is unreachable for 14 consecutive calendar days (as defined in §12.2), the Council automatically enters Emergency Protocol (§12.2). Founder unavailability alone does not constitute grounds for protocol termination._
